Are you a fan of SNL? Do you want to learn how to write like Tina Fey? Do you like laughter and friendship? 
Then come join Tisbert, Carnegie Mellon’s premier sketch comedy troupe! A subtroupe of Scotch’n’Soda Theatre, 
Tisbert provides opportunities for students across campus to create and perform original sketch comedy, 
cumulating in a brand new sketch show every semester. With weekly writing meetings open to all 
Carnegie Mellon students and a new workshop series aimed at developing sketch-writing skills, Tisbert is 
a great place for writers of every skill level. It doesn’t matter if you’ve never written a sketch or 
if you’ve spent your entire life coming up with beat lines - if you like comedy, Tisbert is the place for you!
